Help

With this app, you can easily record which sections of your bike ride you enjoyed (those you'd recommend to a friend) and which sections you’d prefer to avoid next time.

How to record your ride:

  1. Go to the Record page.
  2. Choose your initial state (Good or Avoid).
  3. Tap Start to begin recording.
  4. Press Toggle whenever you want to switch between "Good" and "Avoid."
  5. When your ride is finished, tap End. Your recording will be automatically saved.

How to segment your GPX file:

  1. Download your GPX file from Garmin, Strava, or another app.
  2. Upload your GPX file on the Segment page.
  3. Select the saved recording you'd like to apply.
  4. Press Process.

After processing, you’ll be able to download separate GPX files for your "Good" and "Avoid" segments. You can then use these files with third-party tools like GPX Studio. Please note that if there is no overlap of time segments in the .txt and .gpx files, no map will display and there will be nothing to download.


Happy riding and happy segmenting!

Use at your own risk.
